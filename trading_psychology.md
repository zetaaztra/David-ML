
# 🧠 David Oracle: Trading Psychology & Scenario Guide

Trading is 20% Math and 80% Mindset. This guide helps you navigate the "Mental Fog" when the market moves against your trade.

---

## 📉 Case Study: "The Monday Dip"
**Scenario**: You entered a Bull Spread at 24,647. By Monday morning, Nifty drops to 24,450. You are in a loss. **What do you do?**

### 🥉 The Gambler Mindset (Aggressive Verdict Hunter)
*   **The Golden Rule**: Exit when the **AI gives up**, not when the **Market drops**.
*   **Action**: 
    1.  Check David at 10:30 AM Monday.
    2.  If the Verdict is still **UP** and Confidence is **> 40%**, you **HOLD**.
    3.  If the Verdict flips to **SIDEWAYS** or **DOWN**, you **EXIT IMMEDIATELY**.
*   **Psychology**: You have a "Peace of Mind" wing (your protection leg). You know your Max Loss. Use that safety to allow the AI's math to play out.

### 🥈 The Greedy Mindset (Disciplined Trend Trader)
*   **The Golden Rule**: One red day is a fluke. Two red days are a trend change.
*   **The "Floor" Rule**: As long as David's Confidence is **> 40%**, you ignore the daily noise and hold.
*   **The "48-Hour Protection"**: 
    1.  If David's confidence drops below 40% on Monday, you stay in.
    2.  If it stays below 40% on **Tuesday** at 2:30 PM, you **EXIT**.
*   **The "2-Loss Shield"**: If this trade loses, and your **next** trade also loses, you must stop trading for 3 days. This prevents you from "revenge trading" when the market is in a regime shift.

---

## 🗝️ Core Mindset Rules

### 1. Eyes off the MTM, Eyes on the Signal
Your broker's "Profit/Loss" gauge is designed to make you panic. David's "Verdict" is designed to make you money. If David says the trend is still alive, trust the 10-year backtested math over your eyes.

### 2. The Conviction Floor
Treat **40% Confidence** as your hard floor. 
*   Above 40%: The trend is valid.
*   Below 40%: The AI is guessing.
*   **Never** enter a trade if David is "guessing."

### 3. Acceptance of the Streak
The 5-year audit showed David can lose for **8 days in a row**. 
*   **The Fix**: Keep your position sizes small. If you can survive the 8-day streak emotionally and financially, you will reach the 196% recovery zone.

---

> [!TIP]
> If you find yourself checking David's button more than 5 times a day, you are likely in a **Gambler** mindset. If you want peace of mind, switch to the **Greedy** style and only check twice (9:30 AM & 2:30 PM).
