## 🛠️ Step 2: Step-by-Step Execution Guide

### 1. The 3:15 PM Check
Never trade in the morning. David needs the full day's data. 
- Open David at **3:15 PM**. 
- Hit **Sync Data** twice to ensure you have the latest prices.
- Look for **Stability**: Does David say "UP" consistently across 3 refreshes? If yes, proceed.

### 2. Choosing the Expiry
- **For Spreads (BULL/BEAR)**: Choose the **Monthly Expiry** (or next week). David needs time to be right. Monthly expiries have a **92% historical win rate**.
- **For Iron Condors**: Choose the **Weekly Expiry** (same week). You want to capture rapid Theta decay and get out before the market breaks your range.

### 3. Placing the Trade
- Identify David's **80% Probability Cone**.
- **Spreads**: Buy the leg closest to current price (ATM). Sell the leg at the 90% (for UP) or 10% (for DOWN) line.
- **Iron Condors**: Sell legs just *outside* the 10% and 90% lines. Buy protection 200 points further.

## 🛡️ Step 3: How to Avoid "Bad Trades"
Before you hit buy/sell, check these 3 Red Flags:

1.  **The Flashing Gauge**: If the Verdict flips from UP to DOWN and back to UP in 15 minutes, the AI is "guessing." **CANCEL TRADE.**
2.  **The VIX Jump**: If the India VIX is up more than **10%** today, the AI's "Sideay" prediction is likely to fail. Fear overrides math. **STAY OUT.**
3.  **Low Confidence (<45%)**: David is admitting he is guessing. Don't bet your hard-earned money on a guess.
